Emergency Alerts

Get notified when it matters most.



FamilyHub offers emergency alerts if there has been an accident detected. Using the phones onboard gyroscope, it can track if there has been a suspected auto accident. If the user pairs an apple watch then there is also the option for alerts if the user takes a fall. Lastly, with the emergency ping button on the maps section, an emergency alert can be sent out to family members if someone thinks they might be followed or in danger somehow. When the alert is sent out the family members can instantly call their loved ones phone.

Push Notifications

Let your phone do the worrying.



Whether the user has their phone on sleep, or is on the home screen or in another app, they can keep up to date with their loved ones. Notifications can alert users if someone arrives of leaves home, work, school or any place of your choice. Google map integration can give live traffic updates and provide estimated times of arrival. If parents set up a geofence boundary, notifications can also be sent out if the child leaves the designated zone. Users can choose which notifications they would like to recieve whether it be based on arrival or departure or location based.

Problem: How might we help families connect better, ensure their safety, and organize their lives?

Solution: FamilyHub helps families keep up with eachother in real time by seeing eachother on the map, sends out critical alerts if a vehicile accident is detected, and organizes their lives with a centralized document hub.


● Emphasis on social media marketing on various platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube)

● Offer promotions on these platforms for initial free 14 day trial on premium family bundle, showcase features of the app

● Market the “peace of mind” assurance

● YouTube pre-roll ads with several scenarios of spouses/parents worrying about their loved ones

● Heavily market through YouTube influencers and creators that have our desired audience of women of 30’s and up or that have children.

● Paid sponsorships of influencers and creators to utilize the trust and authenticity of their relationship with fanbase to build brand recognition and expose app to a wider audience.


Looking at our financial projections we will need some serious revenue to come in. We first researched apps that were similar to our idea such as Life 360. On average, it cost between $3000 to $18000 to make a tracking app with features on it. We believe that it would probably be near the $18000 range due to all of the features we would want on the app. We plan on offering a 14 day free trial of our premuim package as a way to draw in users to get them interested, then funding the app with our pro and premium offers that contain additional features.
